Empthi will be a web and mobile platform that will facilitate empathy-based relationships between people with a mutual need for compassion. Reciprocal empathy and heartfelt kindness leading to long term genuine relationships are intended goals of this platform.
Empthi will be open to US citizens as well as those legally seeking permanent recidency in the US
